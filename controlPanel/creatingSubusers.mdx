---
title: 'Add Subusers'
description: 'Add SubUsers to your server.'
icon: "users"
---

Login to the [SanoServers Control Panel](https://panel.sanoservers.com/)<br/>
Navigate to your server's control panel<br/>
Click ``Users`` on the left-hand side of the server's control panel<br/>
Click ``Invite User``<br/>
Enter the email address of the user you wish to invite<br/>
Select the permissions you wish to give the user<br/>
Click ``Invite User``<br/>

The user will receive an email with a link to accept the invitation. Once they have accepted the invitation, they will be able to access the server with the permissions you have given them.
