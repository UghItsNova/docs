---
title: 'Add Subusers'
description: 'Add SubUsers to your server.'
icon: "users"
---

1. Login to the [SanoServers Control Panel](https://panel.sanoservers.com/)
2. Navigate to your server's control panel
3. Click ``Users`` on the left-hand side of the server's control panel
4. Click ``Invite User``
5. Enter the email address of the user you wish to invite
6. Select the permissions you wish to give the user
7. Click ``Invite User``

The user will receive an email with a link to accept the invitation. Once they have accepted the invitation, they will be able to access the server with the permissions you have given them.
