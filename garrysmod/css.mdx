---
title: 'How to Mount CS:S content on your Garrys Mod Server'
sidebarTitle: 'Mounting CS:S'
description: 'Mounting CS:S content on your Garrys Mod server can enhance your gameplay experience. In this guide, we will show you how to mount CS:S content on your Garrys Mod server.'
icon: "teeth-open"
---

1. Login to the [SanoServers Control Panel](https://panel.sanoservers.com/)
2. Navigate to your server's control panel
3. Stop your server if it's running.
4. Click ``Mount CS:S`` on the left-hand side of the server's control panel
5. Click ``Mount CS:S``

Your Garrys Mod server should now have CS:S content mounted. Players will now be able to see CS:S content when they connect to your server.
