<span style={{color: "#15803D"}}>[Written By Whogi | 1 Minute Read]</span>

Login to the [SanoServers Control Panel](https://panel.sanoservers.com/)<br/>
Navigate to your server's control panel<br/>
Click ``Players`` on the left-hand side of the server's control panel<br/>
In this menu you can see all the players that are on your your server<br/>
You can also view and manage your servers Operators, Whitelists, Banned, and see a list of your offline members<br/>

That's it! You have now managed your server players.
