1. Login to the [SanoServers Control Panel](https://panel.sanoservers.com/)
2. Navigate to your server's control panel
3. Click ``Players`` on the left-hand side of the server's control panel
4. In this menu you can see all the players that are on your your server
5. You can also view and manage your servers Operators, Whitelists, Banned, and see a list of your offline members

That's it! You have now managed your server players.
