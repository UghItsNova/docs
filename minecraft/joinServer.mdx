sidebarTitle: 'Join Server'

icon: "play"
---

1. Open Minecraft
2. Click on `Multiplayer`
3. Click on `Add Server`
4. Enter your server's IP address or domain name
5. Click `Done`
6. Click on your server to join

You should now be connected to your Minecraft server. If you have any issues connecting to your server, please contact our support team.
