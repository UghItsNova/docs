icon: "gears"
---

1. Login to the [SanoServers Control Panel](https://panel.sanoservers.com/)
2. Navigate to your server's control panel
3. On the left-hand side, click `Properties`
4. Edit the settings to your liking or even click the manual button on the top right for more control
5. Click `Save` to apply the changes

Your Minecraft server should now have the core settings you desire. If you encounter any issues, feel free to reach out to our support team for assistance.
