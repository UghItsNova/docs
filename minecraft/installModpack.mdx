---
title: 'Installing Modpacks'
description: 'Installing Modpacks on your Minecraft server.'
icon: "box-open"
---

1. Login to the [SanoServers Control Panel](https://panel.sanoservers.com/)
2. Navigate to your server's control panel
3. Click ``Modpacks`` on the left-hand side of the server's control panel
4. Select your desired ``Provider`` for your server type.
5. You can either search for the modpack you want to install or scroll through the list of available modpacks
6. Click The grey ``Download`` button to install the modpack 
7. You will be prompted to select the version of the modpack you wish to install and whether or not you wish to delete existing files
8. Click ``Install Modpack``

That's it! You have now installed a modpack on your Minecraft server.
