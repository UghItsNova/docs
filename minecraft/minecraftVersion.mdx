---
title: 'Change Minecraft Version'
description: 'Change the Minecraft version of your server.'
icon: "code-branch"
---

1. Login to the [SanoServers Control Panel](https://panel.sanoservers.com/)
2. Navigate to your server's control panel
3. Click ``Versions`` on the left-hand side of the server's control panel
4. Select the version you wish to change to
5. Select the minor version you wish to change to
6. You will see two options ``Wipe Server Files`` and ``Accept Eula`` if you wish to wipe your server files or auto accept the eula select it now
7.Click ``Install``

That's it! You have now changed the Minecraft version of your server.
