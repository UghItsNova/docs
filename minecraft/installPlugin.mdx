<span style={{color: "#15803D"}}>[Written By Whogi | 1 Minute Read]</span>

Login to the [SanoServers Control Panel](https://panel.sanoservers.com/)<br/>
Navigate to your server's control panel<br/>
Click ``Plugins Installer`` on the left-hand side of the server's control panel<br/>
Select your desired ``Version``, ``Server Type``, ``Sort by``, ``Category`` and ``Size`` for your server type.<br/>
Enter the name of the plugin you wish to install in the search bar<br/>
Either select ``Versions`` or ``Install``<br/>
